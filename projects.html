Current Projects

Project Visual Novel

The goal of this project was to create a fully fledged visual novel. The VN we are working on is called NGAT (Next Generation Aircraft and Tactics). It's a game set in an alternate universe, in the not too distant future. It will be a novel with many choices and routes. We hope to have a playable demo by Overload (October) 2014. We are also looking for more artists, script writers, composers etc!

Project Vocaloid

Everyone loves Miku! And everyone has the ability to create their own Miku songs. So we thought, why not us too! And thus we are well on our way to having a fully finished song. We need to work on the music now, lyrics have already been completed. Feel free to help out if you wish!

Project ArtBook

It might be possible for us to exhbit a doujin artbook at Overload this year. We will take art submissions from all who are interested in the club! Manga, drawings, fanfic too!
