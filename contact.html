Contact Us

If you want to know more about us, have some questions or even want to sign up please email us on:

attackonpotato@gmail.com

When signing up, make sure to include your University of Auckland I.D Number and if you feel like it, tell us a bit about the Anime and Manga you enjoy. Note: To become a full member of the club there is a $5 membership fee per semester. This covers the costs for all the events, prizes and food that we provide through the course of the year. Although, if you just want to come along and check us out, we don't mind!

If you are already a member of our club, please check out our Facebook page! We also update people of screenings and events on there, plus there is lots of discussion about Anime/Manga related culture.
