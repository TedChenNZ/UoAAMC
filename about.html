About

Founded in the middle of 2013, the University of Auckland Anime and Manga club has been going strong for over half a year now. Two weekly screenings, karaoke and much much more, we are set to become one of the most vibrant, fun and welcoming clubs at the University of Auckland this year! We have many events planned, but right now they're a secret! Kinsoku Jikou Desu!

Our Executive team (club leaders) consists of nine people. Azul and Dana, who are the presidents and co-founders of the club, Thomas the treasurer, Alice the secretary and five general execs: Melissa, Fred, Gareth, Shae and Chuan. It is our job to make sure the club runs smoothly and plan for the awesome events throughout the year. If you have any concerns or suggestions during club meet-ups, seek us out, we are here to help!

Sometimes people ask, "what exactly do you do?" which is a very tough question to answer. We do a huge variety things, that if we were to describe them all here, this page would become a wall of text.. Not fun to read. Here is the summary:

We have bi-weekly Anime screenings, Manga meet-ups, Karaoke events, Quiz nights, Tutorials... Various teams working on everything from Visual Novels to Vocaloid songs (check out the Project Page)

Sound like you, want to join up? Do you have any questions? Head over to our Contact us page and flick us a email, or pop along to one of the screenings listed on this site! (It'd be nice to let us know when you are coming beforehand!)

Our club mascot is Satou-San (designed by Melissa) He is a rabbit, and comes in many different costumes to suit the occasion. Check the Gallery out for more pictures of Satou-san, promotional posters and more!
