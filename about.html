About

Founded in the middle of 2013, the University of Auckland Anime and Manga club has been going strong for over half a year now. Two weekly screenings, karaoke and much much more, we are set to become one of the most vibrant, fun and welcoming clubs at the University of Auckland this year! We have so much planned, but right now it's a secret! Kinsoku Jikou Desu!
